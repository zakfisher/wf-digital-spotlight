Leveraging Social for Acquisition

Acquisition is an extremely important component for any healthy digital marketing program, especially so during the holiday season, when you want to capitalize on increased online traffic. Take a look as some successful campaigns from around the globe and don't forget to check out new changes to Facebook Promotions before you launch your next campaign.

I Love Fountain Gate Competition

Strategy:

Westfield Fountain Gate ran a 're-gram' competition, using a series of competition image with the same (or very similar) competition copy placed over an each image. New competition creative was posted every few days, with regular Instagram images in between promotion posts. Participants were required to tag #ILoveFountainGate and mention @westfieldfountaingate.
